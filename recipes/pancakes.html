Funnel Pancake

This is the crazy funnel-pancake

Warning 1: this meal will break your diet. In fact, reading the recipe may break your diet.

Start with 3lbs of bacon. Layer them in a large pan with tall sides (to minimize mess). Start with temperature at medium until fat starts to render, then raise temperature to medium-high. Turn regularly until bacon is cooked to desired tenderness. This method of cooking sounds like pure blasphemy, until youve tried it. The bacon isn't cooked in the traditional way, but is instead basically deep fried in its own rendered fat. It also doesn't produce that much mess, because the bacon on top blocks most of the "splattering mess." You have to try it.

As the bacon is nearing completion, start mixing up a box of premade pancake mix*. I personally prefer to use milk instead of water, even though you could get by with water in a pinch. Make your mix a teensy bit runnier than you usually would.

Remove the bacon from the pan but LEAVE THE 2-3inches of bacon grease in the pan!

Now, begin dipping pancake batter into the pan, as if you were making a pancake. You'll know the grease is hot enough if the pancake begins separating slightly and cooking instantly. After about 15 seconds, flip the "pancake" over. 15 more seconds and remove.

Here is how you know you've done it right: The results are more like a funnel cake than a pancake. The pancake looks CRAZY. Big holes. It should also be dripping with bacon grease. Also, it should be the most delicious pancake you've ever had. Crunchy on the outside, soft and cakey deliciousness on the inside. You should want to eat five more. After eating the second one you should feel awful about yourself though. You'll likely still eat at least five.

The bacon is also good, btw.
