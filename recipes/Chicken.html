Grilled chicken

The recipe

This is my simple recipe for grilling up a nice juicy chicken for eating.

  • Chicken pieces (breast, thigh, etc)
  • oil - prefer olive oil or butter
  • seasonings of choice (salt, pepper, garlic)

Steps

    Grilled chicken is so simple, even you could do it.

  1. Turn grill on high to pre-heat it.
  2. Coat the chicken in the oil, and then your seasonings
  3. Put your chicken on the grill. Let sit for a few minutes before flipping. Remove from grill when it is cooked to a proper internal temp.
