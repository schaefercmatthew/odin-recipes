Shrimp Scampi

I have no idea how to make Shrimp Scampi, but I'm sure it involves cooking the shrimp, creating the sauce, and cooking the noods.

Steps

    These are the steps to creating a good scamp.

  1. First, get the most delicious shrimps you can find. I'm talking about some mouth watering suckers. Cook 'em good, too.
  2. Then, you have to make a freaking amazing sauce. Like, really good.
  3. Lastly, cook the noods all adente like. That will help them to have the right texture and the right flavors.
